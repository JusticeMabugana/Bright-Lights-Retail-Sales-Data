# Bright-Lights-Retail-Sales-Data

## Introduction

The repository contains data related to customer transactions. It includes details such as transaction ID, date, customer demographics (gender and age), product category, quantity purchased, price per unit, and total amount spent. This data was used for analyzing sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and demographic insights.

## Insights
- May had our highest revenue at R50K.
- Females generated more revenue than males.
- Saturdays are our highest selling days.
- Young adults are our highest group of consumers.
